About Falling Below

About

Falling Below

Falling Below are a rising force in the alternative/modern metal scene, blending massive choruses, crushing riffs, and high-energy live shows.

Featured on Kerrang! Radio, BBC Radio, and TotalRock, their latest single, ‘Answers’, marks a bold evolution with lower-tuned guitars, melodic synths, and falsetto vocals.
